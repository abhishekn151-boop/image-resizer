Frequently Asked Questions

Is this image resizer really free?

Yes — Free Image Resizer is completely free with no subscriptions, no hidden fees, and no usage limits.

Do my images get uploaded to a server?

No. Everything runs inside your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

Will resizing reduce image quality?

Quality only decreases if you lower the compression slider. You have full control over the output quality.

What formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, WEBP, BMP and several other formats are supported. More formats will be added soon.

Does this tool work on mobile?

Yes! The tool is fully responsive and works on Android, iOS, tablets, laptops, and desktops.

Is there a file-size limit?

There is no artificial limit. The only limit is your device’s memory. Most modern devices easily handle large images.

Is this tool safe to use?

Absolutely. Since no images are uploaded, the tool is extremely secure. Everything remains on your device.

Can I use this tool for commercial tasks?

Yes. You are free to use all resized or compressed images for commercial and personal projects.
