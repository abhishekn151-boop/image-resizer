Frequently Asked Questions

Top Questions

1. Is this image resizer really free?

Yes! The tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges, limits, or sign-ups.

2. Do my images get uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens directly inside your browser. Your images never leave your device.

3. Will resizing or compressing reduce quality?

You can control the output quality using the quality slider. Higher quality = bigger file; lower quality = smaller size.

4. What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, WEBP, BMP, and more formats will be added soon.

5. Does the tool work on mobile?

Yes! The tool works on Android, iOS, tablets, and all modern mobiles.

6. Can I resize multiple images at once?

Multi-image batch processing is coming soon.

7. What is the best format for small file size?

WebP usually gives the smallest file size while maintaining good quality.

8. Are my resized images stored anywhere?

No data is saved, logged, or transmitted. Everything stays inside your browser.
