Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Welcome to Online Image Resizer & Compressor. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This tool works entirely inside your browser—no uploads, no storage, and no tracking of your images.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Uploaded images
  • EXIF data
  • Personal data
  • User identification data
  • Cookies related to image processing

2. Information We Collect

We may collect anonymous analytics such as page visits and browser type. This data cannot identify you.

3. Local Image Processing

All image resizing, compressing, cropping, and converting operations run locally on your device. Images never leave your browser.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Cloudflare for secure hosting and optional analytics. These services do not access or store your images.

5. Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.

6. External Links

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
