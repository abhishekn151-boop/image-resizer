Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At Free Image Resizer, your privacy is our top priority. This tool is designed to process all images directly within your browser. This means your images never leave your device, are not uploaded to any server, and are never stored, logged, or shared.

Information We Do Not Collect

We firmly believe that privacy is a right. Therefore, we do not collect:

  • Your uploaded images or output images
  • Personal information such as your name, email, address, or contact details
  • Tracking information such as browsing history or location
  • Device identifiers, metadata, or analytics tied to your identity

How Image Processing Works

All image resizing, compression, conversion, and rendering operations happen locally inside your web browser using modern HTML5 and JavaScript APIs. Your files never reach any external server controlled by us or third parties.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies, trackers, pixels, or behavioural analytics. Some hosting providers may collect basic anonymized statistics (such as page views), but these are not linked to you or your images.

Third-Party Services

Although the tool itself does not send data anywhere, our hosting provider may collect general, non-identifiable server logs. These logs never include images or personal user data.

Children’s Privacy

Free Image Resizer is safe for users of all ages. Because we do not collect personal data, the tool is naturally compliant with COPPA and other child-safety regulations.

Data Security

Since no images or personal data are uploaded or stored, the risk of data theft or breaches is effectively eliminated. All processing happens within the secure environment of your own device.

Your Rights

Because we do not store any user information, there is no data to view, modify, delete, or export. You remain in full control of your images at all times.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally if we introduce new features. Any changes will be clearly mentioned with a revised date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about privacy or how your images are processed, feel free to reach out through our Contact Page.
