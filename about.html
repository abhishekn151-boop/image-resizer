About Us

Who We Are

Image Resizer & Compressor is a modern, privacy-focused online tool created with a simple goal: to make image optimization fast, easy, and 100% secure — without the need to upload anything.

Today, most online photo tools upload your images to servers before processing. We believe your images belong to YOU. That’s why our tool processes everything locally inside your browser. No uploads, no servers, no data collection.

Our Mission

To provide a fast, clean, and secure way for anyone — students, designers, developers, and everyday users — to resize, compress, crop, and convert images instantly.

Why We Built This Tool

  • Existing tools were slow or filled with ads
  • Uploaded images were stored on unknown servers
  • Users needed a fast, safe, private solution
  • Creators needed a simple method to optimize images for web & social media

What Makes Us Different?

  • 100% Local Processing — Images never leave your device
  • No Signup — Use instantly
  • Free Forever
  • Fast & Lightweight
  • Modern UI — Clean, smooth, professional design

Our Vision

To create a complete suite of browser-based media tools that are safe, private, and easy to use — without tracking, without ads, and without compromising your data.

Your support helps us continue improving and adding new features. If you have suggestions or feedback, feel free to reach out anytime.
