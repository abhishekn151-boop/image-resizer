Resize and Compress Images Online — Fast, Free & Secure

Our online image resizer allows you to quickly reduce image size, adjust dimensions, crop photos, and convert formats — all directly in your browser. No uploads, no servers, no data collection. Everything stays on your device.

Key Features

  • Resize by pixels, percentage, or longest side
  • Compress images with adjustable quality or target size
  • Crop with draggable precision
  • Rotate images
  • Convert between JPG, PNG, and WebP
  • 100% offline and private

How to Use

  1. Click the upload area or drag your image into the tool.
  2. Select resize type: pixels, percent, or longest side.
  3. Enter the Target File Size (KB) (required) and choose output format (JPEG/WebP recommended).
  4. Click "Process Image" to resize or compress.
  5. Preview and download the optimized image.
